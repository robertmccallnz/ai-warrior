Whanaungatanga · Kaupapa · Aotearoa me Tāwāhi

Ngā Hoa me Ngā Kaupapa

E kore Te Kiwi Dialectic e haere takitahi. Kei raro nei te whakahaere e tū nei mātou, me ngā kaiako AI taketake, ngā porowhita mātauranga, me ngā kaupapa toi, tuhituhi anō hoki — e pānuihia ana, e tohatohaina ana, e tū tahi ai mātou ki ō rātou mahi. Kei te ora ia hononga i te wā o te whakamātautau whakamutunga.

Te Whakahaere E Tū Nei Mātou
Kaupapa e hangaia ana · Ōtepoti Dunedin

Te Pā

He kaupapa mātauranga tuwhera kua whakaputaina mai i Ōtepoti Dunedin mō te mātauranga AI taketake me te mana motuhake raraunga. Kua hāngai ki Te Tiriti o Waitangi, ki ngā tikanga Māori, me te mātauranga Māori; e tūhono ana ki a CARE me OCAP. Kei te whakatūria he kaitiaki ohu — e tuwhera ana ngā tohutohu hiahia mō ngā tūru kaitiaki / poari tīmatanga. Ko te kaupapa tuakana o Te Kiwi Dialectic — te kāinga o te mahere rhizome me te tikanga whakaputa whakaae mō ngā reo taketake.
