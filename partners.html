Partners & Kaupapa

The Kiwi Dialectic doesn't walk alone. Below is the org we represent and the indigenous AI educators, pedagogy circles, and art & writing kaupapa whose mahi we read, share, and stand alongside. Every link is live as of the last check.

The Org We Represent
Forming kaupapa · Ōtepoti Dunedin

Te Pā

An open educational kaupapa published from Ōtepoti Dunedin for indigenous AI and data sovereignty education. Grounded in Te Tiriti o Waitangi, tikanga Māori, and mātauranga Māori; aligned with CARE and OCAP. A charitable trust is being established — expressions of interest open for founding board / kaitiaki seats. The Kiwi Dialectic's sibling kaupapa — home of the rhizome mapper and the consent-led publishing protocol for indigenous languages.

CARE OCAP Maiam nayri Wingara Te Tiriti-led

01 · Partner Kaupapa

Indigenous AI Educators

Kaupapa Māori and indigenous-led organisations putting tikanga, sovereignty, and community values at the centre of AI design and pedagogy.

Framework · Aotearoa

Dr Karaitiana Taiuru

Released what is described as the world's first Indigenous Peoples AI Framework. Long-standing leadership on Māori data sovereignty and ethics.

taiuru.co.nz→
Iwi-led AI · Waikato

Kaitiaki in the Digital Age

Graeme Smith and Conny Huaki's mahi on Māori-led AI tools — including a custom Kaitiaki o Ngāti Mahuta GPT for environmental policy.

thisisgraeme.me→
Global Network

Indigenous AI

International collective working on indigenous protocols for artificial intelligence. Position papers, frameworks, and cross-nation conversations.

indigenous-ai.net→
Research · Turtle Island

Aboriginal Territories in Cyberspace

Indigenous-led network exploring digital sovereignty, AI, and new media art from Turtle Island. Long-time collaborators on indigenous protocols for AI.

abtec.org→
02 · Partner Kaupapa

Pedagogy Circles

Kaupapa Māori and indigenous education organisations whose pedagogy shapes how we teach AI literacy — ako, whānau, problem-posing, and tikanga-grounded.

Knowledge Base · Aotearoa

KaupapaMāori.com

The reference site for kaupapa Māori research, theory, and practice. Cited in academic and community work across Aotearoa.

kaupapamaori.com→
Education Consultancy · Aotearoa

Te Ara Ki Hawaiki

Kaupapa-first education consultancy working with kura, principals, SLTs, and iwi to centre identity, language, culture, and whakapapa in schools.

tearakihawaiki.squarespace.com→
Tertiary AI · Aotearoa

Ako · AI for Good NZ

Open kaupapa Māori deep-dive resources for tertiary educators integrating AI into teaching. Free, well-maintained, te Tiriti-aligned.

ako.aiforgood.org.nz→
Hauora · Aotearoa

Toi Tangata

Māori physical activity, nutrition, and hauora through a kaupapa Māori lens. Long-running model for community-grounded indigenous education.

toitangata.co.nz→
First Languages · Australia

Australian Indigenous Languages Institute

Peak body for indigenous language revitalisation across Australia. A close kin kaupapa for any AI / language project that respects consent-led protocols.

aila.org.au→
Research · Aotearoa

NZ Council for Educational Research

Independent research org committed to Te Tiriti. Publishes some of the most cited Aotearoa work on AI in primary and tertiary classrooms.

www.nzcer.org.nz→
03 · Partner Kaupapa

Art & Writing Circles

Indigenous and grassroots arts, media, and writing kaupapa — because AI literacy without a literary, visual, and political imagination is just compliance.

Media · Australia

First Nations Media Australia

Peak body for First Nations broadcasting, media, and communications. Models what indigenous-controlled media infrastructure looks like.

firstnationsmedia.org.au→
Storytelling · Turtle Island

Native Bio

Indigenous-led organisation centring Native voices in story, science, and public narrative. Long view on cultural sovereignty in media.

nativebio.org→
Arts Funder · Aotearoa

Creative New Zealand

Kahui Toi Māori panel and dedicated Māori arts strategy. Practical funder of Māori and Pasifika writers, artists, and publishers.

creativenz.govt.nz→
