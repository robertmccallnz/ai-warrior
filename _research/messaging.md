# AI Warrior · trilingual messaging set
Last updated: 2026-06-28

Three languages for the launch kit: **English** · **te reo Māori** · **Tagalog (Filipino)**.

Tone: working-class, direct, no filler. Short enough to scratch into black lacquer.

## Karakia / opening line (te reo only)

For the press kit and the front of each poster series:
> **Whakangungua te hinengaro. Whakangaihia te iwi.**

This is the kaupapa in one breath. Use as the opening inscription on every Hotere-style poster.

## Notes for the designer

- Keep slogans to ≤ 8 words per language wherever possible — they need to scratch into a black field
- Te reo macrons (ā ē ī ō ū) must be preserved at all sizes; render as Unicode, not stripped ASCII
- Tagalog uses Latin script — no special encoding needed but proofread for ñ/Ñ if used
- Avoid Anglicisms in the te reo translations (no "AI Warrior" with English "Warrior" — use "toa")
- Tagalog "Mandirigma" = warrior (literal); "uri" = class (in the socialist sense)
